# Unit Economics

## What Is a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. A typical customer is a startup spending $2,000–$10,000/month on AI tools. Credex offers ~20–30% discounts on these credits.

**Estimating average deal value:**
- Average qualifying team spends: $3,500/month on AI tools
- Credex discount: ~25% → $875/month savings for the customer
- Credex margin on credits: ~10–15% of the discounted price
- Annual credit purchase: $3,500 × 12 = $42,000
- Credex revenue per customer: $42,000 × 12% margin = **~$5,040/year**

**Lifetime value (LTV):** Assuming 18-month average customer lifespan and 85% annual retention:
- LTV = $5,040 × 1.5 years = **~$7,560**

## Conversion Funnel

```
Audit Completed         →  1,000 users/month (target at steady state)
  ↓ (30% email capture)
Email Captured          →    300 leads/month
  ↓ (15% qualify — >$500/mo savings)
High-Savings Flagged    →     45 qualified leads/month
  ↓ (20% book consultation)
Consultation Booked     →      9 consultations/month
  ↓ (40% convert to purchase)
Credit Purchase         →    3.6 customers/month
```

**Monthly revenue from SpendPilot funnel:**
- 3.6 customers × $420/month Credex revenue = **$1,512/month**
- Annual: **$18,144/year** from this single tool

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = $83,333/month in recurring Credex revenue.

At $420/month revenue per customer: need **~198 active customers**.

At 3.6 new customers/month from SpendPilot alone, this would take 55 months — too slow.

**What would have to be true:**

1. **10x audit volume:** 10,000 audits/month instead of 1,000. Achievable with:
   - Hacker News front page (1 hit = 5,000–20,000 visits)
   - Product Hunt launch (3,000–10,000 visits)
   - SEO for "AI tool pricing comparison" (1,000–3,000 visits/month at rank 1–3)
   - Embedded widget on 10+ tech blogs (1,000+ visits/month)

2. **Higher conversion rates:** 25% email capture → 20% qualify → 30% book → 50% convert. Requires:
   - Better audit UX (benchmark mode: "teams your size average $X")
   - Automated follow-up email sequence (3 emails over 7 days)
   - Case studies showing real savings

3. **Higher ACV:** Target mid-market ($10k–$50k/month AI spend) where deal sizes are $50k–$100k ARR. This changes the math dramatically:
   - 20 mid-market customers at $50k ARR = $1M ARR
   - SpendPilot identifies these high-value targets automatically (>$500/mo savings flag)

4. **Multi-channel:** SpendPilot is one of 3–4 lead sources. Combined with:
   - Outbound sales to enterprises identified via SpendPilot data
   - Partner channel with VCs recommending to portfolio companies
   - Conference presence at AI/DevTools events

**Realistic timeline with all levers:** 12–18 months is achievable if SpendPilot hits 5,000+ monthly audits and Credex adds outbound sales for high-ACV accounts.

## Unit Economics Summary

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average deal value (annual) | $5,040 |
| LTV (18-month) | $7,560 |
| Blended CAC | $15–$25 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | **300:1 to 500:1** |
| Payback period | < 1 month |
| Monthly customers from tool | 3.6 |
| Break-even audits/month | ~200 |

The unit economics are extremely favorable because SpendPilot is a zero-marginal-cost lead gen tool (no paid spend, no SDR salaries). Even at conservative conversion rates, each audit completed costs Credex essentially nothing.
