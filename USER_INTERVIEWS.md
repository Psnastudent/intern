## Interview Tips

**Where to find people to interview:**
- College seniors doing freelancing or side projects
- Startup founders on LinkedIn or X (filter by "AI" or "developer tools" in their bio)
- Indie hackers on Twitter or the Indie Hackers community
- Friends, classmates, or relatives who use AI tools for work

**Questions to ask:**
1. What AI tools do you currently pay for?
2. Do you know your actual monthly total spend on AI tools?
3. Have you ever tried to optimize or compare pricing?
4. Which AI tool feels overpriced to you?
5. Would you use a free audit tool that tells you where you're overspending?
6. What would make you trust the recommendations?
7. Would you share the results with your manager or team?

**Reminder:** Credex explicitly states that fabricated interviews are an instant reject. They've read enough fake ones to spot the pattern. Talk to 3 real humans — it's the easiest requirement to fulfill and one of the strongest signals they evaluate.
